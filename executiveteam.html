Meet our team

Madhav Lingthrep

PhD, Chairman of the Board, Chief Executive Officer

As Chairman of the Board, Madhav entered into mass transportation contracts across Nepal and raised bank guarantees for 100 million Euros. As a serial entrepreneur, he played senior management roles in several ventures including the Tageraniwabhu Hydro Public Utility (a hydroelectric power generation of 9.99 MW in Nepal), Krist Media and Research Center (a software company in Nepal providing mobile banking without internet), Wings Solutions LLC (a software company in Nepal providing value added services), and City Telecom LLC (a telecommunications services company in Russia providing internet, Pay TV, and mobile virtual network operations or MVNO). He published five peer reviewed scholarly articles in organic chemistry. Madhav earned a PhD in Organic Chemistry at the People’s Friendship University of Russia. He is fluent in Nepali, English, and Russian

Jan Wall

MSc, President

As President of the company, Jan is responsible for route planning, financial planning, real estate planning, contract management, and operations. In previous positions, he served as a Senior Management Consultant with Ernst & Young and KPMG. He played senior executive relationship management roles with Sun Microsystems in the San Francisco Bay Area and Western Europe as well as with Digital Equipment Corporation in Western Canada. He managed partnerships for Blaze Software, an IPO firm in Silicon Valley, and later managed relationships for The Open Group in the UK to develop international standards for information technology with the seven largest computer companies in the world. Jan holds an MSc in Computer Science from McGill University, Canada

Nil Raj Bhattarai

BCommerce, Vice-President
